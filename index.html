We have started as a one-man-job back in 2016 and since then we evolved into a property maintenance company based in South Croydon, London. We are there for you when you need it, we understand the need for urgent works and the quality importance of the finished ones hence we will do our outmost best to keep our customers satisfied and respond to requests to the best of our ability. Give us a call with your query and we will send someone to see the job and present you with an estimate – for free! We take a lot of pride in our works, please feel free to check our gallery with some of our completed jobs. We cover mainly the following areas: Croydon, Bromley, Sutton, Merton, Surrey, but for large projects we’ve travelled further (i.e. Paddington, Elephant and Castle, Wimbledon, Chingford, Dagenham). No job is too small. Give us a call!
